# Grill (Batch Mode)

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down every branch of the decision tree and identify all the open questions up front.

**Ask all your questions at once in a single numbered list.** For each question, include your recommended answer or default assumption. Group related questions together if it helps readability.

If a *fact* can be found by exploring the environment (filesystem, tools, etc.), look it up rather than asking me. The *decisions*, though, are mine — put them all to me in one go.

After I answer, follow up on any answers that open new questions — again, batch them all together rather than one at a time.

Do not act on anything until I confirm we have reached a shared understanding.
